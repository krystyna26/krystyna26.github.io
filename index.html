ABOUT ME

I’ve recently graduated from Coding Dojo, an intensive 14 week coding bootcamp in San Jose. In 2017, I’ve completed over 1,000 hours of self study and onsite at the bootcamp. In bootcamp, we have learned the fundamentals of Swift, Python (Django & Flask) and MEAN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS) stack.

I am often asked why I picked up programming since my undergraduate & masters are in business, my answer to that lies in some of the shared aspects between the two. In business, understanding logic, math and working with others are key to success, I believe those very same key ingredients are important in programming. Aside from the similarities, coding has been a great way to push and develop myself further.

So you might be wondering what am I up to now? Currently I am practicing algorithms, working on new projects and when I’m not programming I like to spend time outdoors cycling (road) and exploring new places.
